This was another project I completed in the course of learning to use Tableau. It uses publicly available New York State health data and allows the user to dynamically filter key variables by region, severity of injury/ailment and specific injury/ailment type. A quadrant plot was used to show the relationship between cost and length of stay by hospital, serving as a convenient way to identify hospitals that are underperforming in terms of how long patients are staying for injury types, or in terms of total cost of stay.
