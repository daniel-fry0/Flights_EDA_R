Disease and Risk Factor Analysis by Gender

Skills Utilised:

  • R and Rstudio
  • ggplot2
  • Statistical Analysis

This is the second project for my Graduate Certificate in Data Analytics through Sydney University. I found the dataset myself and came up with the brief that I would investigate. It is an investigation into which disease categories if any have a disproportionate impact on one gender over another. It then identifies specific diseases that are responsible for this, and further explores what contribution specific risk factors make in the mortality for this disease. Recommendations are made for the client based on these findings.

HSC Biology Band Prediction Model

Skills Utilised:

  • Python
  • Machine Learning
  • Data Governance
  • Data Wrangling and Cleaning
  • Microsoft Excel

As a passion project alongside my teaching, I was curious whether assessment results through year 11 and 12 could be used to predict HSC band outcomes at the end of year 12. This project was the beginning of a learning journey for me, and taught me the funadmentals of programming, modelling and machine learning. The model uses a random forrest regression and was trained on deidentified student data. The model has a mean absolute error of 1.95 and was deployed as a cloud app for my students to access and see how their current assessments and future assessments might affect their HSC outcome.

Exploratory Data Analysis of Student Assessment Data

Skills Utilised:

Prior to beginning work on the model above, I conducted extensive data cleaning and analysis on student assessment data using excel and python. Length and Cost of Stay Analysis: New York Hospitals

Skills Utilised:

  • Tableau

This dashboard was create in the course of my learning to use Tableau. I used publicly available hospital data to present information regarding how length of stay and severity of injury impact on the total cost of stay. This dashboard could be utilised to identify outlier hospitals or service areas in terms of particularly long stays or particularly high costs for stays. This data would be a good jumping off point for an investiation into hospital efficiency, understaffing levels or underperformance in treatment KPIs.

Google Sheets Training Planner/Dashboard

Skills Utilised:

  • Google Sheets Formula Language

A passion of mine is rock climbing and training for rock climbing. A challenge in my training has always been scheduling and tracking the activities and exercises needed within the week and across multiple training blocks. Using Sheets I created a training planner that can take inputs in terms of exercises and then allows the user to set the number of those exercises per week and schedule and complete them using drop down menus and check boxes. Dynamic visualisations show what percentage of each category has been scheduled for the week and also how much of the weekly training load has been completed. While google sheets may not be industry standard in terms of data management, much of the syntax is transferrable from sheets to Excel, and I consider myself capable in Excel as well.
